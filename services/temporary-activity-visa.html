Temporary Activity Visa

The Temporary Activity Visa (subclass 408) allows applicants to engage in short-stay and short-term activities in Australia. To be eligible for this visa, applicants must possess the necessary skills for the activity, have private health insurance, demonstrate adequate financial support, have genuine intentions of temporary stay, meet health and character requirements, and obtain sponsorship from an approved Temporary Activities Sponsor if applicable. Support from an organisation, Australian citizen, or permanent resident may also be required.

The visa offers various application streams, including invited participants, sports, religious workers, domestic workers, research, exchange programs, special programs, superyacht crew, Australian Government-endorsed events, and entertainment. The length of stay permitted varies depending on the activity, ranging from 3 months for invited participants to 4 years for Australian Government activities.

The Temporary Activity Visa provides several privileges, such as staying in Australia for the nominated period, engaging in the specified activity, bringing eligible family members, and entering and leaving the country while the visa is valid.

Applying for the visa requires sponsorship from an approved sponsor who has passed the sponsorship test. Applicants must fulfil specific requirements, including financial support, health insurance, activity criteria, and meeting health and character standards.

Sponsorship for the visa can be provided by various entities, such as Australian organisations, government agencies, foreign government agencies operating in Australia, religious foundations, and super yacht captains or proprietors.

Additional requirements include meeting health and character standards, providing evidence of financial capacity, and demonstrating functional English proficiency.

The visa validity period depends on the approved activity, ranging up to 4 years for invited participant and Australian Government-endorsed activities, and up to 2 years for other activities.

Family members can be included in the visa application, provided that the sponsor agrees to sponsor them and the intended stay is longer than 3 months.
